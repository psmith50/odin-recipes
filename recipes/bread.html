Bread

Sliced white bread
  • 360g flour
  • 240g water
  • 6g salt
  • 1/12th teaspoon yeast
  1. add all ingrediants into bowl and let it sit
  2. fold dough and put in fridge to rise overnight
  3. preheat oven to 475f in the morning
  4. bake 1 hour with lid on, then 10 minutes with lid off
  5. enjoy
