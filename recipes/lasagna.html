lasagna

Creamy and delicous lasagna pasta with cheese and red sauce
  • 2 lbs pasta
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • parmasean cheese
  • garlic
  • onion
  1. make red pasta sauce and simmer for 30 minutes
  2. preheat oven 400f
  3. cook pasta in boiling water
  4. drain and lay out strips in baking dish
  5. add cheese, put in oven and bake
  6. enjoy
